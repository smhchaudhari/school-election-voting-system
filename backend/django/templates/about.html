How it works
1) Candidate Registration
Students apply with details & manifesto for approval.
2) Voting
Voters cast their vote during the official window.
3) Results
Results are published after voting ends.
Guided Election Hub

A single dashboard that shows what’s open right now and what actions are available — no clutter, no confusion.

One Account, Two Modes

Login once. Then choose how you want to participate: continue as a Candidate or continue as a Voter.

Reliable & Transparent

Built for fairness: controlled election phases, clear rules, and results published only after voting ends.

Participation roles

Everyone signs in the same way. What you can do depends on the current election phase and your chosen mode.

Candidate
Apply & submit manifesto

During registration, candidates submit details for approval. After approval, they appear for voters.

Voter
Vote during the window

Voting is enabled only during the voting phase. Each voter can vote once (party-based voting).

Admin
Create & control phases

Admin schedules elections, approves candidates, and ensures the process moves smoothly through phases.
